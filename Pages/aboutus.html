About Milkee Foods Products

Since 2001, Milkee Foods Products has been dedicated to delivering high-quality, tasty, and affordable food products that bring joy to families. From dairy to desserts and refreshing drinks, our focus has always been on creating products that combine freshness, flavor, and value.

Our Products

  • Watalappan
  • Puddings: Biscuits,Custard, Jelly Pudding, Caramel Pudding
  • Yoghurt Drinks
  • Fruit Drinks
  • Sweets items

History

  • 2001 – The Beginning: Milkee Foods was established with its first product – yoghurts, which quickly gained popularity.
  • Early 2000s – Expanding Sweetness: Introduced the traditional favorite watalappan, loved for its authentic taste.
  • Mid 2000s – Growing Variety: Expanded into a range of desserts including biscuits, pudding, custard, jelly pudding, and caramel pudding.
  • 2010s – Refreshing Beverages: Launched yoghurt drinks and fruit drinks, adding freshness and variety to our product line.
  • Today: With over 20 years of experience, Milkee Foods Products continues to grow, innovate, and serve customers with a wide variety of delicious and affordable foods.

Leaders

Milkee Foods Products began as a small, passionate venture with just one dedicated employee – the founder himself. From the very beginning, he poured his heart into crafting high-quality and delicious products, laying the foundation for a trusted food brand.

Over the years, his vision, dedication, and leadership transformed the company into a thriving business. As the business grew, leadership was passed on to his elder son, who continued to expand the company’s product range and market reach.

Today, under his guidance, Milkee Foods Products has flourished into a household name, delivering a wide variety of tasty and affordable foods to families across the region.

At the heart of Milkee Foods’ success is strong family leadership, blending experience, passion, and innovation to drive the company forward while staying true to its roots.
